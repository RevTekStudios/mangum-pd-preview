COMMUNITY CONNECTION

Building stronger relationships beyond the badge

The Mangum Police Department is committed to visible, positive engagement with the community through leadership programs, public events, seasonal activities, and partnerships that bring citizens and law enforcement closer together.

Featured Program

Project S.T.R.O.N.G.

Project S.T.R.O.N.G., led by Major Josh Olson, is a summer leadership program focused on teaching young people what service, character, and community leadership look like in action.

The program includes mentoring, speakers, training opportunities, and community service projects designed to inspire youth to become role models and positive influences in Mangum. The 2025 program was held in June and remains one of the department’s key community efforts.

COMMUNITY EVENTS

Moments that bring the community together

From seasonal celebrations to unique public events, the department works to be present in ways that are visible, memorable, and community-minded.

Seasonal Event

PD vs FD Snowball Fight

One of the department’s standout community events was a snowball fight between the police and fire departments that also welcomed participation from the community.

Fall Tradition

Halloween at Oklahoma & Pierce

The department helps coordinate Halloween activities with the city and other departments at the intersection of N. Oklahoma Ave and Pierce St, creating a fun and visible community event.

Community Presence

Halloween Parade

The Mangum Police Department also participates in the local Halloween parade, showing support for community traditions and helping make the event memorable for local families.

WHY IT MATTERS

Community engagement is part of public safety

Positive interaction builds trust, strengthens relationships, and helps create a stronger connection between the department and the people it serves.

Visible, approachable policing

Community events help citizens see the department not only as a law enforcement agency, but as a local partner invested in the well-being of Mangum.

Programs with lasting impact

Leadership and outreach programs like Project S.T.R.O.N.G. are designed to leave a lasting positive influence on youth, families, and the wider community.

Halloween in Mangum

Youth Leadership

Project S.T.R.O.N.G. focuses on leadership, service, discipline, and community responsibility. From training and mentoring to service work and food support efforts, the program is designed to challenge youth to grow as leaders.
