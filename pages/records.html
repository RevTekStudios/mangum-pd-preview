RECORDS & INFORMATION

Public records, reports, and information requests

The Mangum Police Department supports public access to information and works to comply with Freedom of Information requests as allowed by law. This page provides a starting point for individuals seeking reports, accident information, and other public records.

Contact the Department View Services
Public Access

Committed to openness and lawful access

The department believes in being accessible to the community and serving as an advocate for lawful public access to records whenever possible. Requests for information, reports, and other documents are handled in accordance with applicable law and department process.

COMMON REQUESTS

Information people often ask for

This page can help guide citizens toward the right starting point when requesting public information.

Reports

Police Reports

Citizens may request information about available police reports and other public-facing records through the department and city process.

Accidents

Accident Reports

Individuals seeking copies of accident-related documents can contact City Hall and ask to speak with an officer regarding report availability and the next steps.

Information Requests

Freedom of Information Requests

The department is committed to complying with Freedom of Information requests as allowed by law and can help direct citizens through the process.

HOW TO START

Requesting records or report information

For records, accident reports, and related public information, citizens should begin by contacting City Hall.

Step One

Contact City Hall

Visit or contact City Hall at:
130 N. Oklahoma Ave, Mangum, OK

Step Two

Ask to speak with an officer

Let City Hall know you are requesting information about a report, accident report, or other public records matter and would like guidance on how to obtain copies.

Step Three

Follow the request process

The department will work to explain the process, available options, and any lawful limitations that may apply to the requested records.

Important Note

Availability may vary

Some records may not be immediately available or may be restricted by law, depending on the nature of the report and the information requested.

Need Help?

Questions about records or report copies?

Start with City Hall at 130 N. Oklahoma Ave, Mangum, OK and ask to speak with an officer about obtaining copies of reports, accident reports, or other public records.
